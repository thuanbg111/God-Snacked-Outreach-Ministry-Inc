Privacy Policy

This policy explains how God-Snacked Outreach Ministry Inc. (“we”, “our”) handles information on this website. We design our site to be simple and privacy-friendly, especially on mobile devices.

Summary: We only collect information you provide in the contact form and basic technical logs to keep the site secure. We do not sell your data or use third-party advertising trackers.

Last updated:

Scope

This policy covers this website and any pages under our domain. It does not cover third-party sites we link to (e.g., Google Maps).

Information we collect

  • Contact form — your name, email, and message content when you submit the form.
  • Operational logs — limited technical data (e.g., IP address, browser type, date/time) to operate and secure the site.

How we use information

  • Respond to inquiries and provide support;
  • Operate and improve the website, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot;
  • Comply with applicable laws for nonprofit record-keeping.

Cookies & tracking

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising cookies. Essential cookies (if any) are only used to keep the site working.

Sharing & disclosure

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We may share information with service providers (e.g., hosting) strictly to run this site, under confidentiality obligations.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, users, or the public.

Data retention

We keep contact form messages only as long as needed to respond and for basic nonprofit records, then delete or anonymize them.

Your choices & rights

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of information submitted via the contact form.
  • You can opt not to submit the contact form and instead email us directly.

To make a request, use the contact details on the Contact page.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children

This website is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children online without appropriate consent where required.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
