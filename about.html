About God-Snacked Outreach Ministry Inc.

God-Snacked Outreach Ministry Inc. is a California Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation in McKinleyville, CA. We feed and provide for the needs of the homeless.

Mission

  • Provide meals and food assistance to people experiencing homelessness;
  • Distribute essential care items and seasonal supplies;
  • Offer consistent outreach and connection to resources.

Public Benefit

Organized exclusively for charitable purposes within the meaning of IRC §501(c)(3).

EIN: 87-3488453

Governance & Compliance

  • No private inurement to directors, officers, or members;
  • No political campaign activity or substantial lobbying;
  • Upon dissolution, assets go to another §501(c)(3) organization.
